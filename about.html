About Nimbus

Nimbus Cloud Slides were created with a simple idea: everyday footwear should feel as soft as walking on clouds, without looking bulky or cheap. We wanted slides that you can wear to the gym, on a flight, or to grab food — and still feel put together.

Our Approach

Every pair of Nimbus slides is built around comfort, durability, and clean design. We use high-quality EVA foam to keep each step cushioned while staying lightweight. The shape is designed to support your foot and reduce pressure during long days.

Instead of chasing complicated designs, we focus on a few essential colors and a streamlined silhouette. That makes Nimbus slides easy to style with almost anything in your closet.

Built for Real Life

  • Slides you can wear after sports, at home, or outside.
  • Easy to clean — just rinse or toss in a gentle wash cycle.
  • Slip-resistant soles to handle wet locker rooms and rainy days.
  • Durable foam that keeps its shape instead of flattening out immediately.

Why We Started

Nimbus began from noticing a problem: most budget slides either feel terrible after a week or fall apart fast. On the other hand, premium brands charge a lot more without always delivering real comfort. We wanted to bridge that gap — slides that feel premium, look clean, and stay affordable.
