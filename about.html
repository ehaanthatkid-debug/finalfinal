About Nimbus

Nimbus Cloud Slides were created with a simple idea: everyday footwear should feel as soft as walking on clouds, without looking bulky or cheap. We wanted slides that you can wear anywhere.

Our Approach

Every pair of our slides is build for comfort, durability, and a clean and minimalistic design. We use high-quality EVA foam for cushioning while staying durable.

Instead of complicating the designs, we focus on a few essential colors and a clean look. That makes Nimbus slides easy to style with anything in your closet

Built for Real Life

  • Slides you can wear after sports, at home, or outside.
  • Easy to clean — Machine-Washable
  • Slip-resistant soles
  • Durable foam

Why We Started

Nimbus began from a problem: most budget slides either feel terrible after a couple days or fall apart quickly. On the other side of things premium brands charge a lot without even delivering true comfort. We wanted to bridge that gap, by creating slides that feel premium while staying afforable.
